• How quickly will my order be dispatched?

      We aim to dispatch any orders made before 14:00 the same day, otherwise they will be dispatched next working day.

  • Are your products really sustainable?

      Yes! We only work with suppliers who are approved by the Global Organic Textile Standards certified, so you can be sure our clothing is sustainable and eco friendly. Check out our ‘Why Organic’ page for more information.

  • What if I haven’t received my order?

      If you haven’t received an order, please contact us or send us a DM on Instagram, and we’ll look into it.

  • How do you package your products?

      Our clothing is sent loosely folded in compostable, plastic free mailing bags, which can be disposed of in household food waste. Our tags are recyclable, as is the hemp twine used to attach it.

  • Can I change an order I’ve already placed?

      If you need to change an order, please let us know as soon as possible through the contact us page.
